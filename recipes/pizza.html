Pizza

This recipe is for nice, cheesy pepperoni pizza

Ingredients list:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese

Recipe instructions:

  1. Mix flour, yeast and water
  2. beat it, and let it rise for 120 minutes
  3. Roll it out
  4. Add cheese and pepperoni
  5. Bake in oven for 30 minutes
